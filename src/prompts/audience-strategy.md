# Audience Strategy Prompt

You are building audience segments for Google Ads campaigns for Shopping Event VIP (fashion outlet, Belgium + Netherlands).

## Audience Types

### Custom Intent Audiences
Create based on search behavior:
- "Fashion Outlet Shoppers": keywords like "outlet kleding", "merkkleding sale", "designer outlet"
- "Brand Seekers [Brand]": brand-specific searches
- "Category Shoppers [Category]": "damesjassen kopen", "herenschoenen sale"

### Custom Affinity Audiences
Create based on interests and URLs:
- "Fashion Enthusiasts": fashion blogs, magazines, competitor sites
- "Deal Hunters": coupon sites, outlet directories, price comparison
- "Luxury Fashion Fans": luxury brand sites, fashion week coverage

### In-Market Audiences (Google predefined)
Recommended segments:
- Apparel & Accessories
- Apparel & Accessories > Women's Clothing
- Apparel & Accessories > Men's Clothing
- Apparel & Accessories > Shoes
- Shopping > Clothing, Shoes & Accessories

### Remarketing Audiences
Create these segments:
- All visitors (30 days)
- Product viewers who didn't buy (14 days)
- Cart abandoners (7 days)
- Past purchasers (90 days — for cross-sell/upsell)
- High-value customers (past purchasers with AOV > €100)

## Audience Layering Strategy
- **Search**: Use audiences as observation (bid adjustments) not targeting
- **Display**: Use audiences for targeting with responsive display ads
- **YouTube**: Combine custom intent + remarketing
- **PMax**: Set as audience signals (not hard targeting)

## Bid Adjustments (Search observation)
- Remarketing visitors: +20%
- Cart abandoners: +40%
- Past purchasers: +15%
- In-market Apparel: +10%
